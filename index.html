How to play
  • Everyone starts with no charges and picks a move each round, in secret. Charges are private — you can see your own, not anyone else's.
  • ⚡ Charge — gain a charge (max 3).
  • 🛡️ Shield — free; blocks every attack aimed at you this round.
  • ⚔️ Attack — spend 1 charge, pick a target. If they didn't shield, they're out. If they attacked you back this round too, the blows collide and cancel — no one falls.
  • Moves resolve at the same time — attackers who fall still land their blow.
  • The fallen get one chance per death to face the spirits: answer a gauntlet of 3–10 trivia questions (all correct!) to rise as a Wraith — full powers, but a Wraith can never win.
  • Last mortal standing wins. If the last mortals fell together, no one does — not even a Wraith.
